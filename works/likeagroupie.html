Like a groupie incognito posing as a real singer

Score and Performance, 2019

As part of ‘The Sonic Study Series’ at The Reid School of Music, University of Edinburgh, 6.2.20
As part of ‘The same song for a long time’ lecture, Social Acoustics Seminar #1: Sound, Hearing, Movement, Performativity, Faculty of Fine Art, Music and Design, University of Bergen, 10.4.19
As part of ‘Experiments in Standard Time: Part 1’ at Anneke Kampman’s ‘Labour's Own Sounding Ideal’ exhibition, Pumphouse Gallery Battersea Park, 9.3.19

 

Collaboration with John Harries. Intervention into ‘Gods and Monsters’ by Lana Del Rey. On sadness, slowness, and failed imitation. For two performers - voice and electronics, with processed audio and time-stretched music video.
