Performance

 

Channeling the power of the dead with Rutger Hauser Digest

The Clore Ballroom, Southbank Centre, London
5 May 2014, with Ian Stonehouse
 

 

All day fluxus DJ performance using turntables, cassette players, dictionaries, pens, paper, furniture and other artefacts with the power to channel the dead.

Rutger Hauser Digest are a splinter faction of the larger ensemble Rutger Hauser.
