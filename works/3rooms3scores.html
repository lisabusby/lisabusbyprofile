3 Rooms, 3 Scores

Text Score
Published September 2015, with Wysing Arts and Electra. Riso print, Edition of 4 copies.
 

 

 

Reflecting on my relationship with three domestic spaces in Wysing Arts Farmhouse. Inspired by per-zines, collecting, counting and list making. All editions left ‘on site’ in the three bedrooms and one living space.

The score Room 5 was performed at event “If we’re going to use this wretched term, then let’s be clear who was innovating in it.” at Wysing Arts Centre as part of The Multiverse Electra Residency, 25 July 2015. It was one text of four included in a performance of Lucy Reynolds’ ongoing Feminist Chorus project.
