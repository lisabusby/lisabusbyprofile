I begin alone in this action: a series of sounding zones

Series of outdoor long duration performances
Umeå, Sweden 2014 in group show Survival Kit
 

 

 

 

 

A series of 8 x 8 hour DJ performances in various urban and rural locations around the city of Umeå, without electricity or sustenance.
Commissioned by Survival Kit Festival, and part of the Umeå European Capital of Culture programme 2014.
