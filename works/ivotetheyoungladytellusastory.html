I vote the young lady tell us a story

Installation and long duration performance
The Ashmolean, Oxford, 2013, in show 'Musical Technologies Old and New'
O3 Gallery, Oxford, 2012, in show ‘Wonderland’

A series of original compositions and SFX loosely scoring Lewis Carroll’s Alice stories, are resonated using piezo disc systems through a series of 12 readymades set within a larger installation of ephemera. The resonators are DJ-ed alongside existing cassettes, vinyl and found object sound-makers to create a rich sonic and visual landscape.

This work has existed as both a gallery based installation, and durational construction performance.
