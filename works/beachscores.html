Beach Scores

Text score series, 2018

Untitled beach score #2, performed on Whitstable Beach with Ingrid Plum for ‘Sounding Shore’ at Whitstable Biennale, 2.6.18
Song of Resentment [for Brighton Beach], commissioned for Ingrid Plum’s Taut, experimental vocal score collection and accompanying album of performances. Debut performance 11.3.18

 

 

Performance experiments for coastlines. Concerned with bodily endurance, working with and submitting to the elements; of entanglements and contrasts of close proximity/physical distance, miked/acoustic sound, immersions in sea/sand, and static/moving voices.

Untitled beach score #3 as yet unperformed.
