Two people in a clock by the digital sea

Sounding objects
O3 Gallery, Oxford, 2012, in show ‘Wonderland’
 

Original compositions emit from hacked vintage music boxes, replacing the existing and expected sonic contents, and as such perceptions of the objects’ function. This interactive series of music boxes can be performed or ‘DJ-ed’ by the visiting audience.
