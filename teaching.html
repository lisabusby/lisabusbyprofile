Working for 10 years in higher education and beyond across fields of composition, popular music, sound, and performance. From songwriting, electronic music production, audio collage and sampling, to experimental, fringe and underground musics and live art, Lisa has extensive experience in teaching creative practice tools and methods for developing musicians and artists. She has taught in a range of educational settings: at universities in the UK and internationally, regularly at Goldsmiths University of London; and with music organisations and charities including The Albany Deptford, Roundhouse Camden, School Ground Sounds Brixton, Music for Youth, and The Creative Society. She offers 1-1 mentoring and private tuition for developing artists.
